About Shree-AI

Shree-AI is a robust, open-source personal assistant built with the latest AI models like GPT-4 and GPT-4 Vision. With the ability to understand natural language, analyze images, and perform tasks based on user inputs, it's the ideal tool to boost your productivity. Although still in development and facing some limitations due to the lack of GPU resources, we're actively working on improving the system, especially with the **LOEON.Ai** beta model, which will offer even more advanced capabilities, including internet access.

Features of Shree-AI

Chat Mode

Engage in dynamic conversations with Shree-AI. Whether you're tackling complex queries or brainstorming ideas, Shree-AI offers instant, meaningful responses tailored to your needs.

Vision Mode

Analyze images and videos in real-time with Shree-AI's advanced vision capabilities powered by GPT-4 Vision. Get quick insights from photos, diagrams, and video content.

Speech Synthesis

Convert text to lifelike speech with support for Azure, Google, and OpenAI's speech synthesis models. Enjoy a seamless voice interface for better interaction with Shree-AI.

File Interaction

Upload your documents and have Shree-AI extract data, summarize content, and answer your questions. Supported file formats include PDFs, DOCX, JSON, and more.

NLP & Vosk Integration

Shree-AI integrates **Vosk** for speech-to-text functionality and **Hugging Face NLP models** for powerful language understanding. These models enable Shree-AI to handle a wide variety of tasks with ease.

Custom Commands

Create custom commands to automate your daily tasks. Whether it's running a Python script, controlling your system, or managing files, Shree-AI adapts to your workflow.
