About Shree-AI

Shree-AI is an open-source personal assistant powered by cutting-edge AI models like GPT-4 and GPT-4 Vision. Built to run locally on your machine, Shree-AI handles a wide range of tasks such as natural language processing (NLP), real-time image analysis, speech synthesis, and much more. However, we are still in the early development stages and are working on improving performance, especially with the lack of GPU resources at the moment. Nonetheless, we are continuously improving Shree-AI's functionality and building the **LOEON.Ai** model, which will offer additional capabilities, including internet access and more advanced features in the future.

Features of Shree-AI

Chat Mode

Shree-AI can engage in natural conversations, handle complex queries, and provide intelligent responses in real-time. This makes it the perfect companion for productivity, whether you're brainstorming or solving problems.

Vision Mode

Analyze images and videos in real-time with GPT-4 Vision. Whether you're working with photographs, diagrams, or videos, Shree-AI can help you extract useful information instantly.

Speech Synthesis

Convert any text into natural-sounding speech using AI models from Azure, Google, and OpenAI. Perfect for accessibility, or simply interacting with Shree-AI in a more natural way.

File Interaction

Shree-AI can interact with various file types, such as PDFs, DOCX, JSON, and more. Upload your documents, and Shree-AI will analyze them and provide you with meaningful insights.

NLP & Vosk

Shree-AI leverages **Vosk** for speech-to-text conversion and Hugging Face's **NLP models** for advanced language processing tasks. Whether it's understanding user input or processing complex documents, Shree-AI handles it with ease.

Custom Commands

Personalize your Shree-AI experience by creating custom commands that automate daily tasks. Whether it's running Python scripts, opening applications, or managing files, Shree-AI adapts to your needs.
